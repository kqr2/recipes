Ingredients:
 - 1 TSP peanut butter
 - 1 TSP jelly (strawberry, etc.)
 - 2 Slices bread

Directions:
  1. Toast each slice of bread
  2. Apply peanut butter to one slice; jelly to the other
  3. Carefully place the two pieces of bread together such that the peanut butter and jelly come into contact
